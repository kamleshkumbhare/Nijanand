CPVC Pipes

Popular CPVC Pipe Products


CPVC Pipes or Chlorinated Polyvinyl Pipes are obtained by chlorination of Polyvinyl Chloride. CPVC has better physical and mechanical properties than PVC, which makes it ideal for Hold and cold water plumbing. CPVC pipes at Nijanand have an unbeatable strength and high resistance to corrosion, industrial chemicals, heat and electrolytes. Our CPVC Pipes are Tough, strong, resilient, durable and long lasting. They are heat resistant upto 93’ celsius.
Our CPVC Pipes are used in varied industrial applications like for conveying hot corrosive fluids & chemicals, water, oils, foodstuffs, gases, sewage water, etc. from one point to another. Further, these are also used for discharge pipe systems, central heating, etc. Some Features that make our CPVC Pipes stand out include it’s light weight, high floor characteristics, smooth inner wall, resistance against acidic & alkaline solutions, and flexibility.
